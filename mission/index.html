What we’re about

We build and ride freak bikes, share tools, and teach each other metalwork, repair, and creative problem-solving. Parades, races, late-night tinkering — all welcome.

Membership

  1. Build a freak bike. Tall, long, cargo, swing — make something wild and rideable.
  2. Make colors. Patch, stencil, or iconography on a vest/shirt/bag. Represent the crew.

Inclusive of all styles.

Weekly Builds

Open shop night for welding, grinding, and brainstorming. Bring a frame or just come learn.
